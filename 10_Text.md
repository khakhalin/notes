# Texts and Language

**Basic methods**
* [[ngram]] - N-grams: low-level feature for text analysis, beyond single words. "Bags" of several (2-3) words.
* [[tfidf]] - Text frequency - Inverse document frequency. A basic ranking approach for text relevance.
