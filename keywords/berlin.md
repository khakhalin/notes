# Berlin


A guide to city events

Field Notes Berlin Calendar
URL: https://field-notes.berlin/en/kalender
A curated calendar dedicated to improvised and experimental music events. Features concerts with unusual instruments, historical reconstructions, contemporary sound art, and world music performances. Updates regularly with avant-garde music happening across Berlin venues.​

Echtzeitmusik Berlin
URL: https://www.echtzeitmusik.de/kalender
The definitive calendar for Berlin's improvised and experimental music scene (echtzeitmusik means "real-time music"). Covers free improvisation, sound art, and acoustic experiments at venues like Labor Sonor, exploratorium berlin, and other underground spaces.​

Synthesizer Museum Berlin
URL: https://www.synthesizermuseum.info
Not just a museum but a venue hosting regular concerts and events featuring electronic music, synthesizers, and experimental sound. Check their events calendar for workshops, guided tours, and unusual electronic music performances.​

ART at Berlin
URL: https://www.artatberlin.com/en/calendar-for-vernissagen-exhibitions-events/
Comprehensive calendar of gallery openings (vernissages), finissages, and special art events. Color-coded system makes it easy to see what's opening when. Covers contemporary art galleries across all Berlin districts.​

Index Berlin
URL: https://www.indexberlin.com/events/list/
Focuses on contemporary art exhibitions, performances, and art-related events. Particularly good for finding experimental and cutting-edge art projects in independent galleries and alternative spaces.​

The Berliner - Art Exhibitions Guide
URL: https://www.the-berliner.com/art/berlin-exhibitions-whats-on-now/
Weekly updated guide to current exhibitions from Museum Island to underground art spaces. Includes opening dates, locations, and admission prices.​

Meetup.com - Berlin Tech Groups
URL: https://www.meetup.com/find/?location=de--Berlin&source=EVENTS&keywords=tech
Search for: "Coding Berlin", "Berlin Robotics Meetup", "GDG Berlin", "Berlin Data Science"
Multiple active tech communities including Coding Berlin (general development), Berlin Robotics Meetup (robotics and AI), Google Developer Group Berlin, and various language-specific meetups (Python, JavaScript, etc.).​

MotionLab Berlin Events
URL: https://motionlab.berlin/events/
Hardware, deep tech, robotics events and meetups. Hosts Berlin Deep Tech Nexus and other innovation-focused gatherings.​

Freie Universität Berlin - Open Lecture Halls (Offener Hörsaal)
URL: https://www.fu-berlin.de/en/sites/offenerhoersaal/index.html
Free public lecture series covering topics from philosophy and religion to biodiversity and Middle Eastern culture. Most lectures in German, but some in English. No registration required.​

ICI Berlin Events
URL: https://www.ici-berlin.org/events/
Independent research center hosting public lectures, performances, conferences on humanities topics. Usually free and open to public. Topics range from academic religion to urbanism and cultural theory.​

Humboldt University Public Events
URL: Check current semester at https://hu-berlin.de (search "Ringvorlesung" or "öffentliche Vorlesung")
Look for "Ringvorlesung" (lecture series) or "öffentliche Vorlesung" (public lecture). Universities are public spaces; you can often attend large lectures without registration.​

Rausgegangen Berlin
URL: https://rausgegangen.de/berlin/
Comprehensive daily event recommendations covering concerts, exhibitions, theater, parties, and niche cultural events. Curated selections help filter through Berlin's overwhelming options. Available as app and website.​

Tip Berlin
URL: https://www.tip-berlin.de/event/
Daily event tips and weekend highlights covering everything from underground clubs to mainstream culture. Strong coverage of free and affordable events.​

Visit Berlin Event Calendar
URL: https://www.visitberlin.de/en/event-calendar-berlin
Official Berlin tourism calendar with filtering by category (music, family, festivals, lectures, etc.). Surprisingly comprehensive including many free events.​

Gratis in Berlin
URL: https://www.gratis-in-berlin.de/
Specifically focused on free events in Berlin. Perfect for budget-conscious explorers. Covers free concerts, exhibitions, festivals, and street events.​

Stressfaktor
URL: https://stressfaktor.squat.net/
Calendar for leftist subculture and politics in Berlin. Essential for finding events at squats, autonomous centers, political talks, Küfas (solidarity kitchens), underground concerts, workshops, and community projects. If you want the truly alternative side of Berlin, this is it.​

Wochenmarkt-Flohmarkt.de
URL: https://www.wochenmarkt-flohmarkt.de
Comprehensive flea market calendar for Berlin. Search by district and date to find ongoing markets like Mauerpark (Sundays), Arkonaplatz, Boxhagener Platz, and more.​

Carnica Spectaculi - Medieval & Historical Events
URL: https://www.carnica-spectaculi.de/veranstaltungen/
Organizer of historical markets, medieval festivals, and knight spectacles in Berlin and Brandenburg. Includes the Historical Christmas Market at RAW-Gelände, Oster-Ritterfest at Zitadelle Spandau, and other medieval events throughout the year.​

Visit Berlin - Events for Children
URL: https://www.visitberlin.de/en/events-for-children-in-Berlin
Filtered calendar specifically for family-friendly events including children's museums (ANOHA), theater performances, concerts, and educational activities.​

Berlin mit Kind
URL: https://berlinmitkind.de/veranstaltungskalender/
German-language family event calendar with extensive coverage of kid-friendly activities, workshops, and events across Berlin.​

Demonstrations.org
URL: https://demonstrations.org/en/events
Comprehensive calendar exclusively dedicated to finding demonstrations, strikes, and rallies across Germany with Berlin coverage. Good for browsing upcoming events by date.​

Critical Mass Berlin
URL: https://criticalmass.berlin/ and https://criticalmass.berlin/termine
Monthly recurring bike demo that takes over streets for cycling advocacy. Happens every last Friday of the month at 20:00 (plus first Sunday of month at 14:00 at Brandenburger Tor). The most established recurring bike protest.​

Fridays for Future Berlin - Climate Bike Demos
URL: https://fridaysforfuture.berlin
Climate activism with bike-related actions. Organizes bike demos specifically against highway expansion (A100).​

Other resources

* Resident Advisor (https://ra.co/events/de/berlin) - The go-to for electronic music and club events​
